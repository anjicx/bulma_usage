Based on the first book in the series, this film follows the adventures of the Pevensie siblings as they discover the magical land of Narnia and join forces with Aslan to defeat the White Witch.

The second film adapts the fourth book in the series, where the Pevensie siblings return to Narnia to aid Prince Caspian in his quest to reclaim the throne from his evil uncle.

The Voyage of the Dawn Treader (2010): Based on the third book, this film follows the adventures of Edmund and Lucy Pevensie along with their cousin Eustace as they join King Caspian on a seafaring journey.
